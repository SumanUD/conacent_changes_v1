About Conacent

Conacent is an enterprise transformation partner helping organizations improve operational performance by connecting business processes, technology investments, and enterprise ecosystems.

We work with operationally complex organizations across manufacturing, retail, logistics, services, and multi-location enterprises to modernize ERP environments, strengthen procurement capabilities, automate workflows, and create integrated operating models designed for sustainable growth.

Rather than approaching transformation as a series of isolated technology projects, we help enterprises build connected environments where systems, teams, suppliers, and information work together to support greater visibility, agility, and scalability.

Organizations engage with Conacent to maximize the value of existing investments, simplify operational complexity, and prepare their businesses for future growth initiatives.
